First Contact

About the Timeline

This timeline chronicles the major events of the Forsarr War Campaign. Each event represents a significant moment in the narrative, often resulting from actual tabletop battles and their outcomes.

The timeline will be updated as the campaign progresses, with new events added to reflect the evolving conflict in the Forsarr System.
