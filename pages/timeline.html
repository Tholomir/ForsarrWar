Campaign Timeline

Forsarr War Campaign Timeline

043.012.M42
The Battle for Druscar's Throne

A mysterious transmission draws all factions to Druscar's Ruin, where a battle for a supposed throne unleashes an ancient horror.

Imperial Chaos Ork Xenos

Imperial, Chaos, and Ork forces clash for the lost throne. The Red Skulls, 201st Cadian, Death Guard, and Slaanesh/Nurgle daemons battle as ancient seals shatter, unleashing a C'tan. Blackstone monoliths erase daemons, and reality unravels as a new horror awakens in Druscar's ruins.
032.012.M42
The Fall of Kelthorn II

Kelthorn II falls to the Dark Mechanicum, becoming a heretical forge-world fueling Chaos war across the Forsarr Sub-Sector.

Dark Mechanicum Chaos

With Arcturus' ascension to Archmagos Diabolus, loyalist Mechanicum are annihilated. Kelthorn II now produces daemon-infused war machines and corrupted Skitarii, spreading blasphemous technology throughout the void.
028.012.M42
The Kelthorn Heresy

A Sensory Nexus beneath Kelthorn II sparks a crisis in the Adeptus Mechanicus, leading to a daemonic invasion and insurrection.

Mechanicus Chaos Slaanesh

Cohort Omnis-9 faces a doctrinal crisis as Slaanesh's daemonic legions descend. Tech-Magos Dominus Arcturus, corrupted by the Nexus, betrays his kin, triggering a techno-daemonic insurrection and opening a Warp rift.
027.012.M42
The Battle of Druscar's Ruin

Catastrophic fall of an Imperial stronghold as the 368th Cadian Brigade and Garnet Swans are overwhelmed by Orks and Tyranids, leaving the ruins to xenos dominion.

Imperial Ork Tyranid

The 368th Cadian Brigade and Garnet Swans made a valiant last stand against Bogggoff's Goff Goggaz and Tyranid Incursion Roh-Epsilon-245. The stronghold fell, and Druscar's Ruin became a xenos-infested wasteland.

About the Timeline

This timeline chronicles the major events and pivotal battles of the Forsarr War, a brutal conflict raging across the Forsarr Sub-Sector. Each entry marks a turning point in the struggle between Imperial defenders, Chaos warbands, xenos invaders, and traitorous forces vying for dominance.

Events are shaped by the outcomes of tabletop battles and campaign decisions, weaving together the fates of worlds, heroes, and horrors unique to the Forsarr setting. The timeline is updated as the war unfolds, capturing the evolving narrative and the impact of every major clash.
