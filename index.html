About the Campaign

The Forsarr System, once a prosperous Imperial holding, now stands at the precipice of total war. Multiple factions vie for control of this strategic sector as ancient evils awaken and new threats emerge.

Follow the progress of our Warhammer 40k campaign, track faction movements, and witness the fate of the Forsarr System unfold through battle reports and narrative developments.
