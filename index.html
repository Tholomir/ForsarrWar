About the Campaign

The Forsarr War campaign is a sweeping narrative of ambition, betrayal, and survival. As ancient horrors awaken and alliances are forged in desperation, the fate of entire worlds hangs in the balance. Every commander's story weaves into the grand tapestry of war, where legends are born and destinies shattered.

From the haunted ruins of Druscar to the corrupted forges of Kelthorn II, the campaign chronicles the rise and fall of heroes and villains alike. The drums of war echo across the Forsarr Sub-Sector—will you answer the call?

The saga of Forsarr is written in blood, hope, and ruin. Your choices will shape its legacy.

Campaign Rules

Campaign Structure

The campaign unfolds over three escalating phases. Players are assigned to alliances, each vying for dominance through a series of linked battles. At the end of each phase, alliances earn Campaign Points based on their victories and achievements.

Resources & Filaments

Throughout the campaign, players collect Kelthorian Filaments—rare, warp-tainted resources. Filaments can be pooled within alliances to unlock powerful bonuses or converted into Strategic Points, which are crucial for determining the campaign's final outcome.

Victory & Legacy

The alliance with the most Strategic Points at the campaign's end claims ultimate victory. Every battle, every resource, and every decision shapes the fate of the Forsarr Sub-Sector—will your alliance rise to glory or fall into oblivion?
